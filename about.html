The Platform

Daata Editions commissions artist video, sound, poetry and web. This new and innovative way to collect art is designed specifically to be a native platform to a new generation of artists who work with moving image and sound. Limited edition artworks can be viewed and acquired as digital downloads.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following credit & debit cards: VISA, Mastercard, American Express.

The prices on the website are in usd. Can i pay in a different currency?

No. Daata Editions website accepts payments only in USD. If you wish to pay with a card linked to a non USD account, your bank will make the conversion to complete the transaction. You can refund an edition within 7 days from the date of purchase. Refunds are not accepted, though, if you have screened or downloaded the edition. No exchanges are available through the Daata Editions website.

Can i make a gift purchase?

You can purchase an edition as a gift to someone by selecting the ‘Buy as a gift’ option during the buying process and providing the name and email address of the person you would like to receive it. Daata Editions will notify the recipient of your gift via email and they will have to open an account or log in to an existing one and commit to the Terms of Use and Conditions of Purchase in order to accept the gift.

How can i subscribe to the daata editions newsletter?

You can subscribe to the Daata Editions Newsletter by selecting the ‘Newsletter’ option and providing your email address at the Info section of the website.

What is a Daate Editions?

Daata Editions is a platform for collecting artists’ video, sound, poetry and web based artwork. Dedicated to supporting artwork online, Daata Editions commissions works available to view and acquire as digital downloads in a limited edition.

What do I get when I Purchase an edition?

When you purchase an edition you get a digital file of the work which is stored in your account on the website and which you can download to your devices (laptop, tablet, mobile phone etc). All editions are accompanied by a Certificate of Authenticity.

How can I purchase an edition?

If you would like to purchase an edition, click ‘Buy’ and the first available edition will be issued to you. As soon as you complete the transaction, you acquire a digital file wc you can download to your device and which is kept online in your account together with a Certificate of Authenticity. How can I have my videos on a continuous loop?

To play your videos on a continuous loop, go to My Gallery, select the video you want to play, click on the play icon and then on the loop icon at the end of the play bar.
